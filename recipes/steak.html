Steak

Description

This recipe will lead you to create the perfect cooked steak. It will be tasty, buttery, seasoned, and juicy by the time you're done. Steaks are a favorite of mine to cook. It's easier than it looks as a lot of the time waiting is having it marinate in your seasonings. Once it's on the pan, you should be done in no time. I'll even explain my secret to a perfect steak in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Season steak with your favorite seasoning, let it marinate for at least an hour or up to overnight in the fridge
  2. Under medium high heat, melt 3 tablespoons of butter in caste iron pan
  3. When butter is melted, add steak to pan. Let first side get a good sear. About 5 minutes
  4. Flip steak. Baste seared side with melted butter in pan. Let unseared side get seared, another 5 minutes
  5. Flip steak. Baste other side and lower heat to medium
  6. Add garlic cloves, rosemary, and thyme to pan for about 3 minutes
  7. Set oven to 350 degrees
  8. Throw steak in the pan in the oven for about 4 minutes
  9. Take out of oven and set aside
  10. Enjoy
